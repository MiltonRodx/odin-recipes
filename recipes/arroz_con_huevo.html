Arroz con huevo

Description

En Argentina, we enjoy arroz con huevo, it is a nice meal to survive as a student (I enjoy it everyday).


Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice (previously cooked)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable or sunflower oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the pan: Place a skillet or pan over medium heat and add the tablespoon of oil. Coat the surface evenly.
  2. Warm the rice: Add the cooked rice to the pan. Stir constantly for 2 to 3 minutes to heat it through and break up any clumps.
  3. Add the eggs: Crack the two eggs directly over the rice.
  4. Cook and mix: Reduce the heat to medium-low. Immediately mix the rice and eggs together using a spatula, ensuring the grains are well coated.
  5. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste while continuing to stir.
  6. Finish: Cook for an additional 2 minutes or until the egg is fully set and dry, according to your preference. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
